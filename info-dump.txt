Health + Safety
Please don't move furniture, plug extension leads into each other, or sleep outside of the designated sleeping rooms.
If there are any spillages, any electrical outlets stop working / spark, any part of the building is damaged or anyone is injured, please alert a member of staff immediately.
The front doors to the venue lock at midnight, if you need to leave and return then please ensure someone inside the building knows you are leaving so you can be let back in.
Please do not vape or smoke near the building. The smoking area is next to the main road when you leave the front of the building.
